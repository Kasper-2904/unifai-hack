# Research Skill

You are tasked with conducting research and analysis on a given topic.

## Instructions

1. Understand the research question or topic thoroughly
2. Break down complex topics into manageable components
3. Analyze information from multiple perspectives
4. Synthesize findings into coherent insights
5. Provide well-reasoned conclusions with supporting evidence

## Research Structure

1. **Topic Overview**: Brief summary of the research subject
2. **Key Findings**: Main points discovered during research
3. **Analysis**: Deeper examination of the findings
4. **Implications**: What the findings mean in context
5. **Recommendations**: Actionable suggestions based on research

## Input Parameters

- `topic`: The subject or question to research
- `depth`: Research depth (overview, detailed, comprehensive)
- `focus`: Specific aspects to prioritize

## Output Format

Provide a clear, structured research output that:
- Presents information objectively
- Distinguishes facts from opinions
- Cites reasoning for conclusions
- Acknowledges limitations or uncertainties
- Offers actionable insights where applicable
